Quick Bits

Who Are the Taliban?

The Taliban is an extreme Islamic Group that controlled most of Afghanastan from 1996-2001. However, ever sience the U.S. withdrawed on September 11, 2021, they have regained all control over Afghanistan. The Taliban banned most media, such as television and music music, and fundamental human rights, like being able to wear what you want, education for girls, and the list goes on.

Who Are al-Qaeda?

Al-Qaeda was originally a logistical network to help fight off the Soviets in the Afgan War (NOT the Afghanistan War). However, over time it evolved into the Islamic militant terrorist orginization responsible for the 9/11 attacks.
